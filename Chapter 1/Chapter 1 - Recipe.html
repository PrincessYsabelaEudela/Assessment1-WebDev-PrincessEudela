Crepe

Crepe is a thin and delicte French-style pancake and can be paired with many fillings, it can be savory like cheese, ham and herbs or it can be sweet like fruits, chocolates and jams. This is a basic crepe recipe, which can be quickly made with 7 ingredients within 1 hour and can be prepared a day in advance.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Method

  1. Combine melted butter, flour, salt, milk, eggs, water and vanilla extract in a large mixing bowl or blender.
  2. Whisk well or blend for about 30 seconds, until the batter is silky-smooth
  3. Cover the bowl and chill in the refrigerator for 30-60 minutes or up to 1 day.
  4. Heat pan on medium heat and grease the pan with butter, make sure to grease the pan with butter between each crepe.
  5. once the skillet is hot, pour 3-4 tbsp of batter into the center of the pan.
  6. Tilt or twirl the pan to cover the pan with the batter, the thinner the better.
  7. Cook the crepe until lightly browned on the bottom, 30 to 45 seconds.
  8. Flip the crepe and cook until lightly browned on the second side, 10 to 15 seconds.
  9. Transfer the cooked crepe to a plate then repeat the cooking process.
  10. Serve with desired fillings.
